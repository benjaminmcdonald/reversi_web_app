Reversi is a board game played by two players on a board with 8 rows and 8 columns. Jump over your oppoent's pieces to turn them into your color. The small dark circles on the board show the places that you can move. The objective is to have more pieces than your opponent when no more moves can be played. The four corners of the board are the most valuable positions to have. Give it a try and if you are still not getting it you will have to ask google for help :-) Reversi is a fun and challenging game that will strength your mind.
